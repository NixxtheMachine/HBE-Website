Maxillofacial Consultant: What Is It We Do?

What Does a Maxillofacial Consultant Do?

Facial & Jaw Surgery

Treatment for jaw problems, facial injuries, facial asymmetry, and structural concerns requiring specialist surgical care.

Trauma & Reconstruction

Management of facial trauma, fractures, and complex reconstruction with function, safety, and appearance in mind.

Aesthetic & Lesion Care

Consultant-led aesthetic procedures and skin lesion management with careful planning, clear communication, and supportive aftercare.
